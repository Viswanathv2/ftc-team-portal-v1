Team Story

How Architechs grew through robotics

Our Journey

We are The Architects, a local robotics team from Mechanicsburg, Pennsylvania, and a passionate group of young innovators with 8 years of robotics experience.

Our journey began with early robotics programs, progressed through FIRST LEGO League, and led us to the World Championship, where we earned recognition at the international level.
