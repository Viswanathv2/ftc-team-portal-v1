Sponsorship Opportunities

Architechs is actively seeking sponsorship from local businesses and community organizations. Your support directly enables our young team members to compete, learn, and grow in FIRST Robotics.

What Your Sponsorship Supports:

  • Robot design, building, and competition costs
  • Educational resources and training materials
  • Travel and competition registration fees
  • Mentorship and STEM learning opportunities

Sponsorship Levels

Platinum Sponsor – $2000+

  • Logo on robot
  • Logo on team shirts
  • Social media recognition
  • Logo on engineering portfolio & pit display
  • Invitation to team events

Gold Sponsor – $1000+

  • Logo on pit display
  • Social media recognition
  • Acknowledgment in team documents

Silver Sponsor – $500+

  • Social media recognition
  • Listed as a supporter on our materials
  • Friends of the Team – Any Amount
  • Thank-you mention on social media
